Safety Systems Required for Gasoline Loading

⚡
Emergency Shutoff
Driver-side front shutoff valve — automatically seals on rollover or collision impact.
🔒
Manhole per Compartment
Separate manhole access required for each tank compartment per DOT-406.
💨
Vapor Recovery System
Prevents vapor loss and controls internal pressure during loading and transit.
🛡️
Rollover Protection
Engineered to prevent manhole opening during rollover events — mandatory requirement.
〰️
Anti-Surge Baffles
Internal baffles limit liquid movement, reducing center-of-gravity shift during transit.
🔺
Hazard Placards
Class 3 flammable liquid placards visible from all 4 sides — regulatory requirement.
